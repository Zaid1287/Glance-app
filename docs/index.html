Is it done yet?

Glance tracks long-running tasks on your Mac — downloads, builds, renders, training runs — and shows their progress on your iPhone as a Live Activity. No remote desktop. No walking back to the machine.

How it works

Macauto-detects the task
→
Encrypted LANChaCha20-Poly1305
→
iPhoneLive Activity + Watch

Only task metadata leaves the Mac — never your files or command output — and it's end-to-end encrypted with a key only your two devices share.

Zero-config detection

Browser downloads, npm/pip/cargo/docker/rsync and more — detected automatically. No scripts, no webhooks.

Live Activity

A progress bar on your Lock Screen and Dynamic Island, with a haptic and a "Done" card that sticks when it finishes.

Apple Watch

The same Live Activity appears in your Watch Smart Stack — glance at progress from your wrist.

Private by design

Loopback by default, end-to-end encrypted, metadata-only, no telemetry. Your data stays on your devices.

Menu-bar agent

Lives in your Mac menu bar like a good utility. One click to copy your pairing key.

Anything else

Wrap any command: glance run --progress '([0-9]+)%' -- python train.py and watch the bar fill on your phone.

Get Glance

Mac

One command in Terminal:

curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Zaid1287/Glance/main/install.sh | sh

Installs the menu-bar agent and starts it at login.

iPhone

Build it to your iPhone with a free Apple ID, or sideload via AltStore.

About

Glance is a small, open-source utility built for one job: the "is it done yet?" moment. It's not remote desktop and not a server monitor — just a fast, private glance at whatever your Mac is working on.
