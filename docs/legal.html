Privacy Policy

Glance collects nothing about you. There is no account, no analytics, and no server that we operate. Everything Glance touches stays on your own devices; the small amount of data that moves between your Mac and your phone is end-to-end encrypted and travels directly over your local network.

Information we collect

None. We operate no servers, accounts, or analytics, and we do not collect, receive, store, sell, or share any personal information or usage data. We cannot see your tasks, files, device identifiers, location, or activity.

Information processed only on your devices

On your Mac, Glance reads — and never transmits to us — in-progress download file names and sizes in ~/Downloads, the running process list (to recognize long tasks), and, for a wrapped command, its output scanned in memory for a progress number and then discarded. When you pair a phone, only task metadata (name, kind, state, progress, timestamps, exit codes) is sent directly to your own paired device over your local network. File contents, full command output, keystrokes, screen contents and browsing history are never transmitted.

Storage & deletion

A pairing key is stored on each device (the Mac key file is user-only; the iPhone key sits in the app's protected container). Unpairing in the app, or deleting the key on the Mac, removes it. Task data is ephemeral and is not written to any database by us.

Third parties & children

No third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. Glance uses only Apple's on-device features (local networking, Live Activities, local notifications), governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. Glance is a general-audience developer utility, not directed to children, and collects nothing from anyone.

Data & Compliance

For reviewers and regulated users: Glance has no server and collects no personal data, so most obligations that apply to data-collecting apps do not arise.

Roles

Because no data reaches us, we are neither a data controller nor a processor of your personal data. You are the sole controller of any data Glance produces, all of which stays on hardware you control.

GDPR (EU/EEA/UK)

We perform no processing of personal data, so no lawful basis, DPO, or representative is required on our side. Access, rectification, erasure, and portability are exercisable directly by you on your devices (unpair to erase the key; task data is ephemeral). No international transfers occur, because data never crosses a network we operate.

CCPA / CPRA (California)

We do not collect, sell, or "share" personal information as defined by the CCPA/CPRA. There is nothing to opt out of.

Apple App Store privacy

Glance's App Privacy declaration is Data Not Collected. The app uses on-device features only — Local Network, Live Activities, and local notifications. Encryption is standard ChaCha20-Poly1305 used solely for the app's own sync (ITSAppUsesNonExemptEncryption = false; confirm for your distribution).

Retention & security

Task data is ephemeral and not persisted by us; the pairing key is retained on each device until you remove it. With no central store, there is no central breach surface. Data is end-to-end encrypted and the Mac transport is loopback-by-default. Contact: [contact email].
